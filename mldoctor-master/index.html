MLDoctor

In impoverished areas, a large majority of the students, and parents, of a district cannot afford to go to the doctor every time that they think their health is in danger, and it is unrealistic to provide schools with the high-tech, high-cost technology in modern-day hospitals, so we created a cheap, affordable and accurate solution. A sleek and user-friendly website that utilizes a multilayer deep learning neural network tool built with Jupyter Notebook to predict the chances of a patient having a certain disease. The website contains models and algorithms for a variety of diseases that anyone with a computer and internet access can use. This tool can be used by nurses and school technicians on all of the students and even some of the parents who don't have health insurance.

Diabetes Detection

Input data such as glucose levels, BMI, and insulin in the model to receive a percentage chance of having diabetes.
